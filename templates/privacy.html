Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use Spendly. This includes your account information (email address, password), expense entries, categories, and any other data you choose to provide. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data to: display your expense records, generate spending summaries and insights, allow you to filter and search your transactions, and communicate with you about service updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. You can delete your data at any time by accessing your account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal financial data with third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers to host our infrastructure, send emails, or analyze usage patterns. These providers have access only to the information needed to perform their functions and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can export all your data in a portable format through your account settings. If you wish to delete your account entirely, please contact us and we will remove all your data within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Spendly after any change constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example.com.
